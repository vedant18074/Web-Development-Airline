Services of Vista Airlines

  1. In flight Services

    • Food and Beverage: Offering complimentary or purchasable meals, snacks, and beverages

    • In-Flight Entertainment: Providing movies, TV shows, music, games, and sometimes live TV

    • Wi-Fi and Connectivity: Offering internet access and in-flight messaging services.

    • Comfort Amenities: Distributing pillows, blankets, and amenity kits in premium cabins.

  2. Ground Services:

    • Check-In Options: Offering online, mobile, kiosk, and counter check-in services

    • Baggage Services: Handling checked and carry-on baggage, and providing options for excess baggage.

    • Lounges: Access to exclusive lounges with comfortable seating, refreshments, and business facilities for premium passengers and frequent flyers

    • Priority Services: Priority check-in, boarding, and baggage handling for premium and loyalty program members.

  3. Customer Support:

    • 24/7 Customer Service: Providing assistance via phone, email, and social media.

    • Special Assistance: Services for passengers with disabilities, medical conditions, or special needs, including wheelchair assistance and guide services.

    • Lost and Found: Helping passengers recover lost items.

  4. Loyalty Programs:

    • Frequent Flyer Programs: Offering points or miles for flights, which can be redeemed for future travel, upgrades, and other benefits

    • Tier Status Benefits: Providing additional perks like extra baggage allowance, lounge access, and priority services for frequent flyers.

  5. Additional Services:

    • Travel Insurance: Offering insurance options for trip cancellation, health, and baggage protection.

    • Car Rentals and Hotel Booking: Partnerships with car rental companies and hotels to provide package deals or discounts.

    • Custom Travel Packages: Tailored packages including flights, accommodation, and activities.
